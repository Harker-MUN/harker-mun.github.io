About US

Harker’s Model United Nations Club has been running for over 20 years and seeks to provide its members with a fun and memorable experience while debating on fun topics in the way that the real UN delegates do. Through this fun debate, members gain valuable skills in teamwork, speaking, leadership, and diplomacy. In addition to our fun club events, members have the opportunity to attend Model UN conferences with members from other schools, and they may even have a chance to travel to one of the world’s best high-school Model UN conferences in Washington DC!

MUN faculty advisor

Ms. Green has been involved in Model United Nations since she was a college student. She looks forward towards heading the Model UN program for the 10th+ year in a row! Along with Model UN, she is involved with a number of other activities at Harker, including being the AP government teacher and Senior Class Dean.
