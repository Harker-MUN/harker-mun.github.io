About us

Learn. Have Fun.

At Harker MUN, our goal is to cultivate our members as global thinkers and leaders, who are well-informed of current issues and eager to develop solutions. To this end, we organize a variety of fun and engaging workshops that focus on developing speaking and leadership skills. In addition, our signature conference (HMUNC) is a great chance for Harker students to improve their diplomacy skills while experiencing the fun of Model UN!

  • Develop important daily skills like communication and speaking
  • Have fun at our conferences and workshops!
